Vegetable Stir-Fry with Garlic Ginger Sauce

Vegetable Stir-Fry Recipe

This Vegetable Stir-Fry is a quick, healthy, and delicious dish packed with colorful veggies and coated in a savory garlic ginger sauce. Perfect for a weeknight dinner, this dish can be enjoyed on its own or served over rice or noodles.

Ingredients

For the Stir-Fry:
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil (or sesame oil for more flavor)
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 large carrot, julienned or thinly sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snow peas or snap peas
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • green onions, sliced
  • 1/4 cup cashews or peanuts (optional, for garnish)
For the Garlic Ginger Sauce:
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce (or tamari for gluten-free)
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch (to thicken)
  • 1/4 cup vegetable broth or water
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for spice)

Instructions

  1. Make the Sauce:
    • In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, cornstarch, and vegetable broth (or water) until smooth. Stir in the ginger, garlic, honey (or maple syrup), and red pepper flakes if you like a bit of spice. Set aside.
  2. Cook the Vegetables:
    • Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the carrots, bell peppers, and broccoli, and cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    • Add the zucchini, mushrooms, and snow peas, and cook for another 2-3 minutes, until the veggies are tender-crisp.
  3. Add the Sauce:
    • Pour the garlic ginger sauce over the vegetables and stir to coat. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until the sauce has thickened slightly and coats the vegetables.
  4. Serve:
    • Remove from heat and sprinkle sliced green onions and cashews or peanuts on top if using. Serve hot over rice or noodles for a complete meal. Enjoy your delicious Vegetable Stir-Fry!
