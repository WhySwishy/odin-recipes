Chicken Alfredo Pasta Bake

Chicken Alfredo Pasta Recipe

This creamy, cheesy Chicken Alfredo Pasta Bake combines tender pasta, juicy chicken, and rich Alfredo sauce with a crispy topping of golden breadcrumbs and melted cheese. It's a satisfying dish that’s great for a cozy family dinner or for feeding a crowd.

Ingredients

For the Alfredo Sauce:
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup milk (whole or 2%)
  • 1½ cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
For the Pasta Bake:
  • 1 pound pasta (penne, rigatoni, or ziti)
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded or cubed (rotisserie chicken works well)
  • 1½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs (panko for extra crispiness)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pasta:
    • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make the Alfredo Sauce
    • In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
    • Pour in the heavy cream and milk, stirring constantly. Allow the mixture to heat through, but don’t let it boil.
    • Gradually whisk in the grated Parmesan cheese, stirring until smooth and creamy. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Remove from heat.
  3. Assemble the Bake:
    • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked pasta, shredded chicken, and Alfredo sauce. Stir until everything is well-coated with sauce.
    • Transfer the mixture to a greased 9x13-inch baking dish. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella evenly over the top.
  4. Add the Topping:
    • In a small bowl, mix the breadcrumbs with a tablespoon of melted butter. Sprinkle the breadcrumb mixture evenly over the cheese layer.
  5. Bake:
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the breadcrumbs are golden brown.
  6. Serve:
    • Garnish with fresh parsley if desired. Let it sit for a few minutes before serving, then enjoy your creamy Chicken Alfredo Pasta Bake!
